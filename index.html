Our Mission

BVC seeks to foster entrepreneurship by facilitating communication, providing access to resources and creating community.

BVC is a private community of 800+ Duke students, alumni and employees interested in all aspects of entrepreneurship. The group welcomes members from any Duke University school, sector or industry function.
